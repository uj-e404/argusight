01 — Foundation

Brand Overview

Argusight is an open source infrastructure monitoring platform designed to give DevOps engineers and system administrators complete visibility across their entire stack. From Linux and Windows servers to MikroTik network devices, Argusight watches everything so you can focus on building, not firefighting.

Name Origin

Combines Argus (from Argus Panoptes, the all seeing giant of Greek mythology with a hundred eyes) and Sight (vision, monitoring, observability). The name embodies complete infrastructure visibility.

Brand Personality

Vigilant

Always watching. Never sleeping. Like Argus Panoptes with a hundred eyes, we never miss a beat across your infrastructure.

Precise

Clear, accurate data with zero noise. Every metric matters. We surface what's important and filter out the rest.

Trustworthy

The tool you rely on at 3AM when something goes wrong. Consistent, reliable, and always there when you need it most.

Powerful yet Calm

Handles complexity without creating complexity. Deep capabilities behind a clean, intuitive interface.

Brand Voice

Technical but approachable. We speak the language of DevOps without being intimidating. Documentation should be clear, direct, and helpful. No marketing fluff. No unnecessary jargon. Just clarity.

03 — Identity

Logo

The all seeing eye. Geometric, clean, and unmistakable. Use as app icon, favicon, GitHub avatar, and brand mark.

Logo Mark (Icon)
On Dark Background
On Light Background
Wordmark
ARGUSIGHT
All Seeing Infrastructure Monitoring

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the eye icon (the "x" height). This ensures the logo breathes and maintains its visual impact in any context.

Usage Rules

Do

  • Use the logo on dark backgrounds whenever possible
  • Maintain the gold accent on the "SIGHT" portion
  • Keep the eye icon proportions consistent
  • Use SVG format for web, PNG for documents
  • Maintain minimum size of 24px for the icon

Don't

  • Do not rotate or skew the logo
  • Do not change the color split between ARGU and SIGHT
  • Do not add drop shadows or outer glows
  • Do not place on busy or patterned backgrounds
  • Do not stretch or distort the proportions
